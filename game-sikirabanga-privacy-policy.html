Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2026

Thank you for using Sikirabanga, an offline mathematical puzzle game developed by Njukii. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Information We Collect

Sikirabanga does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The app:

  • Does not require user registration.
  • Does not require user login.
  • Does not request personal information.
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data.
  • Does not track user activity.
  • Does not use analytics services.
  • Does not collect device identifiers.

Offline Operation

Sikirabanga is designed to operate entirely offline. The game can be played without an internet connection and does not communicate with external servers.

Permissions

Sikirabanga does not request any device permissions for normal operation.

Children's Privacy

Sikirabanga is suitable for users aged 13 years and above. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Third-Party Services

Sikirabanga does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, social media platforms, or data collection services.

Data Sharing

Since no personal information is collected, no personal information is shared, sold, rented, or disclosed to third parties.

Data Security

Because Sikirabanga does not collect or transmit user information, there is no personal data stored on our servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
